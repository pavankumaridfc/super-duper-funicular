Working With WES

Working with WES is simple and requires no contract or commitment. Academic institutions, employers and other organizations refer their candidates to WES for credential evaluation services just as they would for admissions testing or other external services. Candidates then work directly with us and designate your organization as the recipient of WES Evaluation Reports.

Regional Directors & Academic Services

Regional Directors and our Academic Services team are ready to assist institutional staff with any questions regarding our credential evaluation services and other WES resources.

Download the WES services flyer for more information.

Integrate AccessWES® with Slate

Slate integration automatically uploads completed WES credential evaluations directly into your Slate system, saving valuable time and administrative resources. This free integration is now available for all institutions that have an AccessWES® account and use Slate.
