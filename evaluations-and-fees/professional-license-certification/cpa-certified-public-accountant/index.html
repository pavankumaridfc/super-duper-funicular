Our Recommendation

To apply for the CPA examination, a CPA Supplemental Report is required. View sample report.

The CPA Supplemental Report analyzes your business and accounting courses as required by state boards of accounting.

Many CPA boards require official copies of your transcripts. We recommend selecting the CPA International Credential Advantage Package (ICAP) to fulfill this requirement.  With ICAP, we will store your verified transcripts in addition to your evaluation report for easy sending later.

Note: Please confirm with the CPA Board to which you are applying if a WES evaluation will be accepted.
